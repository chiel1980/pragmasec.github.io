01 / ABOUT

Security that doesn't get in the way.

I'm a security professional with two decades of experience engineering, managing, and facilitating the people behind big, dynamic tech companies. I've spent my career on the side of the systems most people never think about — until something goes wrong.

These days that means setting direction as Head of Product for Cybersecurity Enablement Tooling at bol: deciding where the roadmap goes, what gets built, and how my teams are set up to do their best work. The job has changed shape a few times — sysadmin, security engineer, compliance officer, team lead, product manager — but the instinct underneath has stayed the same: find the control that protects the business without slowing it down.
